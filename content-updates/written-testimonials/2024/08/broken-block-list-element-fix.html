• CFI Written Testimony to the Massachusetts Legislature re: H. 377, August 2, 2024 – ✅
    • CFI’s testimony supported legislation that would prevent unfair practices and deceptive advertising by “crisis pregnancy centers,” which routinely fail to give pregnant patients their full range of options regarding reproductive choice and abortion.
  • CFI Written Testimony to the Governor of Louisiana to Veto HB 71, June 14, 2024 -❌
    • Our written testimony calls on the governor of Louisiana to veto HB 71, which would make Louisiana the first state to require posting the Ten Commandments in every classroom in every public school.
  • CFI Written Testimony to the Florida Senate re: HB 931, March 7, 2024 -❌
    • CFI’s testimony opposed legislation allowing public schools to accept religious chaplains as volunteers to provide student services.
  • CFI Written Testimony to the Indiana House of Representatives re: HB 1137, March 4, 2024 -❌
    • Our testimony argued against a bill that allowed public schools to accept chaplains in student service roles. The bill, additionally, required public schools to permit students “release time” to pursue religious instruction off school grounds during the school day.
  • CFI Coalition Letter to the U.S. Senate Appropriations Committee re: S. Rept. 118-44, February 23, 2024 -❌
    • Our coalition letter opposed a Senate Appropriations Committee report that was extremely deferential to the homeopathy industry and called for the FDA to work hand-in-hand with homeopaths toward federal regulation of their products and services.
  • CFI Written Testimony to the Iowa House of Representatives re: HF 2073, February 22, 2024 -❌
    • CFI’s written testimony argued against legislation that would permit Iowa public schools to hire, or accept as volunteers, chaplains to fill student service roles.
  • CFI Written Testimony to the West Virginia House of Delegates re: SB 280, February 8, 2024 -❌
    • Our testimony opposed a bill that authorized teaching “intelligent design” in West Virginia’s public schools.
  • CFI Written Testimony to the Washington State Legislature re: HB 1455, January 29, 2024 – ✅
    • CFI’s testimony supported legislation to ban child marriage in the state of Washington and to raise the legal age of marriage to 18 years old.
